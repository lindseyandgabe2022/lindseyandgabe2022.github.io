Travel & Information

-->



Hood River, OR is an hour east of Portland along the beautiful Columbia River Gorge.

If flying into the area, we recommend arriving to Portland International Airport (PDX) and renting a car to drive to Hood River (1 hour). Public transportation may also be taken to Hood River from the airport (via MAX Redline and Columbia Gorge Express, 2 hours).
Please note that Uber and Lyft do not operate in Hood River!


The Orchard Hood River

The wedding and reception venue is about five miles south of downtown Hood River. The ceremony and reception will be located on a grass lawn, so consider your footwear carefully! We also recommend bringing a light jacket in case it gets chilly in the evening.

A shuttle will provide transportation to and from The Orchard and Hood River Inn for anyone who prefers not to drive to the wedding. The shuttle will also stop at one additional central downtown location; check back later for a schedule and list of shuttle stops!


Other weekend events:

Thursday evening: Welcome drinks. For anyone in town on Thursday evening, we'll be hanging out at the Cebu Lounge (in the Hood River hotel) between 8:30 and 10PM!


Friday night: After Party. For those who'd like to keep the party going after the reception, head to Whiskey Tango! We'll have the upper mezzanine floor reserved for our party. There will be an option to take the shuttle from The Orchard to the bar in downtown Hood River after the reception.


Saturday afternoon: Food and Wine. We'll be hanging out at the Gorge White House (across the street from The Orchard) for some good food and wine tasting... we'd love to have you join! Check back here for an updated schedule.

Hotels and Lodging

Lodging note: Hood River books up quickly in the summer, so we highly encourage reserving accommodations soon! We have a block of rooms reserved at the Hood River Inn - Best Western.


HOOD RIVER INN - BEST WESTERN
This will be the primary hotel where many friends, family, and wedding party members are staying. We will have a shuttle offering transportation to and from this location for the wedding ceremony and reception, as well as a room set aside for anyone arriving on the 17th who needs a place to leave their belongings before check-in. Rooms can be booked at the hotel website.

HOOD RIVER HOTEL
Hood River's oldest and most popular vintage hotel. Located in downtown Hood River, the hotel is convenient to many restaurants, breweries, and wineries.

To make a reservation, call the hotel directly at (541)-386-1900 or visit their website. The hotel has a 3-night minimum stay for weekends in June.

COLUMBIA CLIFF VILLAS
You're in for a real treat at this hotel! The hotel is seated on a cliff next to a waterfall and looks out onto the Columbia River. Make sure to treat yourself to a waterfront room if you stay here. This place is very special to Gabe and Lindsey - this is where they stayed on the weekend Gabe proposed!

Airbnb and VRBO

Hood River has many fantastic VRBOs and Airbnbs that would be a great option for lodging. Please note that Ubers/Lyfts do not operate in Hood River! If you want to stay in an Airbnb it should be in walking distance to the Hood River Inn or the downtown shuttle stop if you do not want to drive after the wedding.
