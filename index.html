About Hood River, OR

Hood River, OR is an hour east of Portland along the Columbia River Gorge. It is a popular Pacific Northwest destination, as it is home to some of the PNW's best wineries, breweries, hiking, wind-surfing, and fishing. If flying into the area, we recommend arriving to Portland International Airport (PDX) and renting a car to get to Hood River. Please note that Ubers, Lyfts, and taxis are hard to find in the area - please plan accordingly.
